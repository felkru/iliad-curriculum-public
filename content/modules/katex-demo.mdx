<Exercise difficulty={3}>
Compute the gradient of $\mathcal{L}(\theta) = \tfrac{1}{2}\|y - X\theta\|_2^2$
with respect to $\theta$, and identify the closed-form OLS solution.
</Exercise>

<Solution>
$\nabla_\theta \mathcal{L} = -X^\top (y - X\theta)$. Setting to zero gives
$\theta^* = (X^\top X)^{-1} X^\top y$, the OLS estimator.
</Solution>
