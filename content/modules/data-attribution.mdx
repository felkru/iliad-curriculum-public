## Overview

This module introduces data attribution as a tool for AI alignment: how to trace a model's behavior back to the training data that produced it. We walk through the chain of approximations from causality down to tractable first-order perturbation methods, and survey three families of attribution techniques — influence functions (IF), Bayesian influence functions (BIF), and unrolling — along with their limitations.
